========================================
        LIRA KURDI ($LKURD) PAYMENT GUIDE
========================================

1️⃣ BASIC PAYMENT INSTRUCTIONS
------------------------------
- Ensure your wallet is connected to the TON blockchain.
- Verify the recipient's wallet address before proceeding.
- All payments are processed in $LKURD tokens.

📥 To Receive $LKURD:
   - Share your TON wallet address with the sender.
   - Ensure your wallet supports $LKURD tokens.

📤 To Send $LKURD:
   - Enter the recipient’s TON wallet address.
   - Specify the exact amount of $LKURD to send.
   - Confirm the transaction and wait for blockchain confirmation.

2️⃣ TRANSACTION FEES
--------------------
- **Network Fee (TON Gas):** Dynamic (depends on network load)
- **$LKURD Transfer Fee:** 0.1% of the transaction amount (minimum 0.01 $LKURD)

3️⃣ PAYMENT CONFIRMATION
------------------------
- Average confirmation time: **5-15 seconds** on the TON blockchain.
- Verify the transaction hash for status tracking.

4️⃣ SECURITY TIPS
-----------------
- **ALWAYS** double-check recipient wallet addresses.
- Do **NOT** share your private keys with anyone.
- Use only trusted wallets and applications.

5️⃣ REFUND POLICY
-----------------
- Crypto transactions are **irreversible**.
- Lira Kurdi does **NOT** support refunds for incorrect transactions.
- Contact support only for technical issues, not for transaction errors.
